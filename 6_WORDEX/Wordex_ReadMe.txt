
=======Wordex v 1.0 (2023.06.22)=======

"Wordex" is an educational program designed to assist users in extending vocabulary, memorizing country capitals, and accomplishing similar language-related tasks. The program offers two distinct modes of operation to cater to different learning styles:

1. Regular Mode: In this mode, users are presented with a word or phrase and are required to input its corresponding translation. By engaging in active recall, users reinforce their understanding by recalling and typing the correct answer.

2. Letter-by-Letter Mode: This mode challenges users by revealing only a portion of a word or phrase, prompting them to complete the missing part. By piecing together the letters and applying their language skills, users enhance their spelling and word formation abilities.

Upon correctly guessing, users collect points based on the length of the word. The scoring system is designed to reward users proportionally to the effort required to complete the task, fostering a sense of achievement and motivation.

One of Wordex's standout features is its ability to import word sets, referred to as decks, from any text file. This flexibility empowers users to curate their learning experience by incorporating word lists specific to their needs or interests. Whether it's specialized vocabulary or country-specific terminology, users can seamlessly load these decks within Wordex.

In addition, Wordex offers the functionality to save and load user profiles. These profiles encompass all the vital information required for a personalized learning experience, including loaded word sets, individual scores, and progress tracking. By persistently storing this information, users can conveniently resume their language learning journey and keep track of their achievements over time.

The program comes with three decks of Russian-English word pairs and two decks featuring state capitals.

With its comprehensive range of features and user-centric design, Wordex strives to provide a natural and professional platform for effective language learning and skill development.

Wordex - программа для изучения иностранных слов и подобных задач.
Программа работает в двух режимах.

1) Обычный. Пользователь видит перевод слова и должен набрать ответ.
2) Побуквенный. Пользователь видит часть слова и должен написать остальную часть.

За каждое угаданное слово пользователь получает очки, количество которых зависит от числа букв в слове.
Наборы слов («колоды») можно загрузить любые из текстовых файлов.

Одна из ключевых особенностей Wordex – возможность сохранять и загружать профили. Они сохраняют подключённые наборы слов, набранные очки и прогресс игрока.
В комплекте с программой идут три колоды русско-английских пар слов и две колоды со столицами стран мира и США.
